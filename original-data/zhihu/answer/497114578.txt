為什麽美國的胖女生要比中國的胖女生有自信？
<p>首先我們先定個基準，到底人應不應該有自信。</p><p>自信的定義是：</p><p>The concept of<b>self-confidence</b>self-assurance in one's personal judgment, ability,<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Power_%28sociology%29" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">power</a>, etc. One increases self-confidence from experiences of having mastered particular activities.<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Self-confidence%23cite_note-1" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">[1]</a>It is a positive<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Self-confidence%23cite_note-%3A2-2" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">[2]</a>belief that in the future one can generally accomplish what one wishes to do.</p><p>不只自信，甚至所有信心，都是經䌞主義的產品。所以無論對於個人形象也好，判斷也好，自信都是不科學而且只具有誤導性的。</p><p>那麽可以說，不自信才是好狀態。</p><p>那麽到底為甚麽美國的胖女生會進入這個不好的狀態呢。那是因為其實大部分的美國人都在這個狀態的。</p><p>主要是因為他們沒有好好讀書。</p><p><br></p><ol><li>Snyder, C.
