Abby 也可以是男生名字喔

2008-11-11
日常生活

The boy's name Abby a(b)-by, also used as girl's name Abby,
is a variant of Abbey, Abbott (Old English), Abelard (Old German) and Abner (Hebrew),
and the meaning of Abby is "father, priest; noble strength; father of light".
